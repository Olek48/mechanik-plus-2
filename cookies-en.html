Cookie Policy

Last Updated: November 16, 2024
Privacy Contact: privacy@mechanik-plus.com

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, phone) when you visit our website. Cookies help us improve the website's functionality and provide you with a better browsing experience.

How We Use Cookies

Our website uses the following types of cookies:

1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the basic functioning of the website. Without them, the website cannot function properly. They include:

  • Language Preferences: Store your preferred language (Czech, Ukrainian, English)
  • Cookie Consent: Remember your decision regarding the use of cookies

These cookies cannot be disabled as they are essential for the website to function.

2. Analytics Cookies (Optional)

Important: Google Analytics is not currently active on this site. The site contains no active Google Analytics tracking code and will not send analytics data.

In the past, we have considered using Google Analytics to analyze traffic to our website. If it were to be activated in the future, these cookies would help us understand:

  • How visitors use our website
  • Which pages are most popular
  • How we can improve our services

Important Information About Google Analytics (if it were to be activated):

  • Google Analytics would be loaded only if you provide consent through the cookie banner
  • IP addresses would be anonymized - your full IP address would not be stored
  • Data would be used only for traffic analysis and service improvement

Contact Form and Data Processing

We use the Formspree service to process our contact form. When you send us a message through the contact form, the following data is processed:

  • Email Address: If you provide it (optional)
  • Phone Number: For contacting you
  • Name and Message: Content of your inquiry
  • Country: May be determined from your IP address by the Formspree service

We use this data only to respond to your inquiry and provide our services.

Managing Cookies

You can change your cookie preferences at any time:

  • Using our cookie banner, which appears on your first visit to the website
  • By clicking the "Cookie Policy" link in the page footer
  • Through your web browser settings

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through settings. You can:

  • Disable all cookies
  • Allow cookies only from certain websites
  • Delete cookies after closing the browser
  • Block third-party cookies

Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the website's functionality.

Third-Party Cookies

We use the following third-party services that may set cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Only with your consent. More information: Google Privacy Policy
  • Formspree: For processing the contact form. More information: Formspree Privacy Policy

Your Rights

In connection with the processing of your personal data, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure of data
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to object to processing

To exercise your rights, contact us at: privacy@mechanik-plus.com
