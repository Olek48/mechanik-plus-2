3. What Data We Collect

3.1 Data Provided by You

When you contact us through our contact form, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address (optional)
  • Phone number
  • Message/inquiry

3.2 Automatically Collected Data

If you consent to analytics cookies, we may collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time of visit
  • Referring URL
  • Device and operating system information

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Processing inquiries: To respond to your requests and questions
  • Providing services: To schedule and provide our auto service
  • Communication: To contact you regarding your inquiries
  • Website analysis: To improve our website and services (only with your consent)
  • Legal requirements: To comply with legal obligations

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Consent: When you fill out our contact form and agree to processing
  • Legitimate interest: To provide requested services and improve our services
  • Contractual obligation: To fulfill your service request

6. Data Sharing with Third Parties

6.1 Formspree

Our contact form uses the Formspree service to process form submissions. Data submitted through the form is processed by Formspree in accordance with their privacy policy.

6.2 Google Analytics

If you consent to analytics cookies, we use Google Analytics to collect anonymous traffic statistics. Google Analytics processes data in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

6.3 Other Sharing

We do not share your data with any other third parties, except when:

  • Required by law
  • Necessary to protect our rights or safety
  • You have given explicit consent

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary:

  • Contact form: Data is retained for the duration of resolving your inquiry and for 2 years thereafter for record-keeping purposes
  • Analytics data: Google Analytics automatically anonymizes IP addresses and retains data according to settings (typically 26 months)
  • Cookies: According to the expiration period specified in our cookie policy

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access: You can request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: You can request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restriction of processing: You can request restriction of processing of your data
  • Right to data portability: You can request transfer of data to another controller
  • Right to object: You can object to the processing of your data
  • Right to withdraw consent: You can withdraw your consent to processing at any time

To exercise your rights, contact us at: privacy@mechanik-plus.com

9. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. This includes:

  • Encrypted connection (HTTPS)
  • Secure data storage
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Regular security audits

10. International Data Transfers

Some third-party services (such as Google Analytics) may transfer data outside the European Economic Area (EEA). These transfers are conducted in accordance with GDPR legal mechanisms, including standard contractual clauses.

11. Children

Our services are not intended for individuals under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If we discover that we have collected data from a child without parental consent, we will take steps to delete it.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website. We recommend checking this policy regularly.
